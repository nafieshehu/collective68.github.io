What is free software?

Free software is about freedom—freedom from corporations who attempt to control their users and place a stanglehold on the internet. We‘re fighting against that.

Free software allows us to…

  • Run the program for any purpose.
  • Study how the program works, and change it to make it do what we wish.
  • Redistribute and make copies so we can help our neighbors.
  • Improve the program, and release our improvements (and modified versions in general) to the public, so that the whole community benefits.

What’s a collective?

Candlewaster is a collective. In short, this means that what we get out of it is proportional to what we put into it. We’re a non-heirarchal team with equal standing that works together to solve problems and create a more free world.
