What’s a web collective?

Candlewaster is a web collective. More specifically, we’re an LLC where every employee is also a partner, and everyone’s salary is exactly the same. Eliminating hierarchy and giving every worker a stake in the company makes us happier, makes us more excited to work on projects, and gives us a stronger sense of ownership and pride in our work.
